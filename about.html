About Me

  My name is Marissa and I am a certified ACA (Veterinary Assistant) and Durham College Animal Care Program Graduate. I started my career 3 years ago at Dogeden Daycare in Markham. I then began working in the clinic setting where I was exposed to Veterinary Medicine, including before and after surgery care. I currently work full time at Baker Animal Clinic as an Animal Care Aide.



  Ever since a young age, I have always had a passion for animals. When I was a child, I held garage sales to raise money for the humane society and spent countless hours volunteering my time at the local animal shelter. I always knew that animals would be my future!



  I spent years pleading with my parents to get me a dog. When they FINALLY budged, I went and chose which puppy I wanted. Next thing I knew, I fell in love with an 8 week old Shih Tzu. She became the centre of my world. Summers were spent camping in Algonquin park with my new puppy and suntanning with her in the back yard. Princess is now thirteen, enjoying every day even though she is now a senior. Having her as my pet has taught me so much about animals and what it truly takes to care for them! I treat each and every pet I interact with as if they are my own.
