Notes:

*Please make note I only accept cash payment

*Please note I am unable to provide nail trims for more sensitive animals and it would be best for these patients to have nail trims done at a Veterinarian or Grooming Facility where two staff members are able to hold. I do not want to stress out your pets!

*Please note that a $15 holiday fee will be charged per day for all statutory holidays (This includes Family day, Good Friday, Easter Sunday, Victoria Day, Canada Day, Civic Holiday, Labour Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day, Boxing Day and New Years Day). If care is provided over a holiday weekend but not on the holiday, a $15 fee will still apply.
